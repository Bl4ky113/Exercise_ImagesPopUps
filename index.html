Objective of The Exercise

In this Exercise, I will recreate a small showcase of Jazz Pictures in a Jazz exposition. The user can "click" the Pictures, these should create a Pop Up with the image and its figure caption or alt text within. Also when the user "clicks" the pictures, they could zoom in the image in the Pop Up. But this is optional by now
